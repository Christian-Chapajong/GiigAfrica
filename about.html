ABOUT

The Global Innovation Initiative Group (GIIG) is a partnership between Amma Group and Loudhailer, focussed on driving dramatic change via disruptive tech investments in emerging markets.

Loudhailer, South African based innovation engagement agency and leading aggregator of the African innovation ecosystem.

Amma Group, specialists in fund formation, with a proven track record in unique asset classes, namely fancy-coloured diamonds.
